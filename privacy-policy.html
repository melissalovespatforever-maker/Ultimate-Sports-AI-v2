Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how ultimate sports AI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our sports prediction intelligence platform.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Username, avatar selection, and preferences you choose when setting up your account
  • Prediction Data: Your betting history, point balances, and prediction performance statistics
  • Communication: Any messages or feedback you send to us through the app

Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: How you use the app, features accessed, time spent, and device information via Mixpanel analytics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and screen resolution
  • Usage Patterns: Game interactions, prediction selections, and navigation behavior

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our sports prediction intelligence platform
  • Analyze prediction accuracy and enhance our AI algorithms
  • Maintain leaderboards and community features
  • Track app performance and user engagement through analytics
  • Send technical notifications and app updates
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Public Information

  • Username and avatar are visible on leaderboards
  • Prediction performance statistics may be displayed publicly
  • Aggregated, anonymized data may be used for research or analytics

Service Providers

  • Mixpanel for analytics and user behavior tracking
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers for app functionality

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, or to comply with legal processes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure data storage and backup procedures

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specific retention periods:

  • Profile data: Retained while account is active
  • Analytics data: Up to 2 years for service improvement
  • Deleted data: Permanently removed within 30 days of account deletion

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics tracking through app settings
  • Account Deletion: Permanently delete your account and associated data

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use Mixpanel analytics to understand how users interact with our platform. You can:

  • Disable analytics tracking in the app settings
  • Use browser privacy settings to limit tracking
  • Opt out of personalized analytics through Mixpanel's privacy controls

Person's Privacy

Our service is not intended for person's under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from person's under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a person under 18, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during international transfers.
